## Tips on Hashtag Selection

- Use a **mix of niche + geo tags** — e.g. #ibumalaysia AND #momlife AND #parentingmalaysia
- Always include at least 2–3 **Bahasa Malaysia** tags to reach local creators who don't use English tags
- Smaller hashtags (10K–200K posts) often surface micro-influencers better than massive tags
- On TikTok, search by keyword (no # needed) — use the English equivalents as search terms
