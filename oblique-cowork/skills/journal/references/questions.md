# Journal Question Bank

60 questions rotating daily. Selected by: day_of_year % 60 (0-indexed).

Questions span 6 themes: Oblique/Leadership, Personal Growth, Relationships, BrandedbySean, Gratitude/Wins, and the Future.

---

## Questions (0–59)

### Oblique / Leadership

0. What's the one thing you're avoiding at Oblique right now — and what's the real reason you're avoiding it?
1. If you could only keep 3 clients today, who would they be and why?
2. Where is Oblique's biggest bottleneck right now — people, process, or product?
3. What decision have you been putting off that you already know the answer to?
4. Who on your team is showing up strongest right now, and have you told them?
5. What's the most important conversation you've been avoiding at work?
6. Where are you the bottleneck in your own business?
7. What would have to be true for Oblique to hit RM 10M ARR?
8. What's one thing you're doing out of habit that you should probably stop?
9. If you were a new client looking at Oblique from the outside — what would impress you, and what would give you pause?
10. What does winning look like for Oblique this quarter — not in general, but specifically?
11. What's a belief about your business that you're not sure is still true?
12. What would you do differently if you were starting Oblique today?
13. What's the hardest part of being CEO that you rarely talk about?
14. If Oblique had a competitor doing something better than you in one area — what area would that be, honestly?

### Personal Growth / Mindset

15. What habit, if you built it this year, would change the most?
16. What are you most proud of in the last 30 days?
17. Where are you being too hard on yourself right now?
18. What does "enough" look like for you — and are you closer to it than you think?
19. What's one belief you hold that most people around you would disagree with?
20. If anxiety is a signal of unpreparedness — what are you underprepared for right now?
21. What part of your old self are you still carrying that no longer serves you?
22. When did you last feel fully present — not just productive?
23. What would your future self, 5 years from now, want you to focus on today?
24. What's something you know you should do, but keep finding reasons not to?
25. What does your best day look like — and how far is today from that?
26. What's a story you tell yourself that might be holding you back?
27. What would you do differently if you weren't afraid of looking like you didn't have it together?
28. What does success feel like — not look like?
29. Where in your life are you playing it safe when you shouldn't be?

### Relationships

### Gratitude / Wins
