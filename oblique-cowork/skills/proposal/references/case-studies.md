# Oblique Case Study Library

Each case study below can be included in a proposal. When generating a proposal, present
this list to Sean and ask him to select 3–5 most relevant to the client's industry.

Update this file whenever Oblique closes a significant new result worth showcasing.

## CS-01: Alba (Dental Boutique)
**Industry:** Aesthetic Dentistry / Healthcare
**Type:** Brand Identity & Positioning
**Overview:** Alba is an aesthetic dentist brand created in collaboration between Dr. Kayla and Dental Boutique from Australia. The goal was to elevate aesthetic dentistry in Malaysia — positioning the brand towards helping people achieve their dream smiles and become their best selves.
**What Oblique Did:** Worked closely with the client to align business goals and created a brand that completely reimagines what a dentist looks like in Malaysia.
**Results:**
- Outlet is now consistently fully-booked
- Recognised by celebrities and influencers as Malaysia's best aesthetic dentist
**Best for proposals involving:** Healthcare, wellness, premium service brands, lifestyle brands

## HOW TO USE

When generating a proposal, present Sean with this list by client name and industry.
Ask: "Which of these case studies would you like to include? Please pick 3–5 most relevant to [client name]'s industry."
Then build those slides using the case study data above.

Each case study slide follows this structure:
- Left: Client overview (2–3 sentences) + brand logo/image
- Right: Results with specific numbers, plus "What Oblique Did" summary
- Footer: Copyright | Oblique Branding [YEAR]
